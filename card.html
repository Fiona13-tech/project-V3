How to Play Coin Chase
  • Objective: Collect all coins in the arena while avoiding the enemy.
  • Movement: Use arrow keys or WASD to move.
  • Escape Zone: The blue block slows the enemy and gives you a safe spot.
  • Levels: Collect all coins to advance. Each level gets harder.
  • Lives: You have 3 lives. The enemy costs one life when it catches you.
  • Pause: Press P or Space to pause/resume during the game.
